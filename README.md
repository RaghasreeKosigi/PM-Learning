#### 1. Why PMs Need SDLC Knowledge
- Understand how ideas become real products
- Plan timelines based on development models
- Coordinate with engineering, QA, and design teams
- Choose the right SDLC model based on product maturity

#### 2. SDLC Models â€“ PM-Relevant Summary

| Model      | When PM Uses It                         | Why Itâ€™s Important |
|-----------|------------------------------------------|-------------------|

| Kanban    | Continuous flow                          | Bottleneck tracking |
